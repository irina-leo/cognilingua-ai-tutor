* Explorer
* Analyst
* Challenge
* Capstone

Original multi-agent feedback architecture including:

* Mentor
* Critic
* Coach
* Novice
* Diplomat

Explorer → Analyst → Challenge → Capstone

### Educational Content

Tasks, exercises, case studies, scenarios, assessment rubrics and supporting documentation.
